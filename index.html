Our Donate Plan

Your donation will help support children in Bangladesh during this emergency,
to help them survive, and to help rebuild after the floods.

  • Food for a family

    To prepare, pack at least 2,000 calories of food per adult per day. In addition, pack at least one gallon of water per person per day for drinking and sanitation in clean plastic containers.

  • Shelter for a family

    Most of the floods affected people are still displaced and living in temporary shelters. They are living on the embankment, road and open space; need emergency lifesaving shelter assistance.

  • Education for a child

    Your donation also helps to ensure continued education for children, providing learning support like Emergency Education Kits for 6-to-10-year-olds so they can keep studying.
